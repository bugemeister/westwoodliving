About Me

My name is Sandi Westwood and I am a decorator and the owner of Westwood Living.


My love for decorating started at a young age, always moving furniture, painting and re-creating spaces to make things fresh and new. There is no greater satisfaction to me than designing spaces that bring joy.   I believe that an inspired combination of texture and colour can add warmth and depth to any room.


My personal aesthetic is an eclectic mix of Mid-Century Modern, Modern, Shabby Chic and Boho. I thrive on re-imagining spaces and bringing them to life using an aesthetic that reflects your own personality and lifestyle. 


I am excited to work with you to create a space that can truly be called your own.


        -Sandi Westwood
